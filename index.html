Grocery/Q-Commerce Persona

Understanding the vulnerability and workflow of quick-commerce delivery partners.

🎯 Problem Statement
Critical Gap

India's 8+ million gig delivery workers in grocery and Q-Commerce face 30-60% income loss during environmental disruptions. Heavy monsoon rains, extreme heat waves (45°C+), and hazardous air quality (AQI 300+) directly reduce order volumes, delivery capacity, and platform activity — yet zero insurance products exist to protect their daily earnings. Traditional insurance requires manual claims, takes weeks to process, and doesn't cover income loss. GigShield solves this with instant, automated parametric payouts triggered by real-time environmental data.

⚠️ Vulnerability Factors
🌧️ Monsoon Rains

Heavy rain (>10mm/hr) makes roads dangerous, reduces visibility, damages products. Order volume drops 40-60%. Partner earns ₹300-400 instead of ₹800+.

🌡️ Extreme Heat

Heat index above 45°C causes dehydration, heat stroke risk. Partners forced to take breaks, reducing delivery capacity by 35-50%.

💨 Poor Air Quality

AQI above 300 causes respiratory issues. Prolonged outdoor exposure is hazardous. Partners reduce working hours, losing 30-40% income.
